Project Title

Product:  Ticketbutler.io |Platform:  Desktop

Originally Ticketbutler system allows organiser to sell additional products alongside with tickets. However, the additional product cannot have different attributes such as color, size. The task is to design a feature for attribute picker on the checkout app.

This project is started from stakeholder interview to underestand the issue and get insights about it. I use the Design Thinking process, the methodology to help the design team and people who are involved in the process to efficiently work together. The most important part is not only the UX research part but also the UI development where there is a certain fuctionality of the feature to focus on.
