Mr.Green Animation

Product:  Det Grønne Køkken & Thai Take Away |Platform:  Video/SoMe Ad

Det Grønne Køkken & Thai Take Away is a franchised restaurant in Denmark. They want to strengthen the brand through its mascot called 'Mr.Green'. The animation tells a story about Mr.Green who got inspired from his journey around the world and exploration of local food from places he has been. Finally came the restaurant where he brings different kind of food to serve people in Denmark!
