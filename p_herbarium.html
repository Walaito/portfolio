Herbarium

Product: Herbarium Tea Packaging|Platform:  Print

Herbarium is an organic herbal tea brand, which they’re proud to provide fine teas to 11 countries in Southeast Asia, reflecting the permeating appeal of their craft. As they would like to present how it easy to drink herbal tea, therefore the brand identity, logo and packaging are simple and nature-care..

The logo represents the brand identity and its concept. The 'H' stands for 'Herbarium' and the leaves and vines mean the organic ingredients. I play a bit with the color by using gradient green, which helps the logo more outstanding and it also means different age of tea leaves.

There are two sizes of the package, sachet and jar. However these are not the final packages, the product team is still on development process to make it more sustainable and green. The logo was approved and done, and it now uses for the brand identity.
